02 : About me

Design, Coffee & Running

My name is Alden. As a kid I would draw to make ideas, thoughts, and dreams come to life. I loved the reactions of my friends and siblings when I would make them laugh at my cartoons and bring a smile to their face. As I grew older I dabbled in digital art and animation, with the same love of seeing my ideas come to life. I decided to dive deeper into learning game design and coding. I created my first game in 2020 about a small knight which is when I launched Lone Knight Games. I continued to develop my coding and design expertise when I got to Wheaton College where my work gained traction. I became the go-to guy to create t-shirts, sweatshirts, stickers, and mugs for campus groups and got to see my work in public. When I began freelancing, I saw my communication, illustration, and graphic design skills bring a client’s vision to life.

Outside of graphic design and game development, I enjoy a good cup of coffee, the outdoors, running, and sports in general.
